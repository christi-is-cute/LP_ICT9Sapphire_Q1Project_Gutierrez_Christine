Barbie is an 11-inch-tall plastic doll with the figure of an adult woman that was introduced on March 9, 1959, by Mattel, Inc. Ruth Handler, who cofounded Mattel with her husband, lead the introduction of the doll. Barbie’s physical appearance was modeled on the German Bild Lilli doll, a risqué gag gift for men based upon a cartoon character featured in the West German newspaper Bild Zeitung.
